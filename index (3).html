How the 12 swatches are made: your exact hex stays untouched at the base stop — every stop before it is generated lighter, every stop after it darker. Perceived (recommended) spaces the steps by how bright they actually look to the human eye, so the ramp feels smooth and even. Linear spaces them by raw HSL numbers instead, which can look uneven for some hues.

The Perceived mode is inspired by and built on the OKLab color space designed by Björn Ottosson, used here with attribution.
